The hosting provider of the website automatically logs certain information, which your browser shares automatically with us. These are:

Cookies

This website uses cookies. Cookies are small text files, that your web browser saves on your device. Cookies help us to provide a better experience on our website.

Some cookies are "session cookies". Those cookies will be deleted automatically after your browser session ends. Other cookies might remain on your device, until you delete those manually. Those cookies help us, to recognize you, when you visit our website again.

With a modern web browser you can monitor, restrict or deny the usage of cookies. Most web browsers allow the configuration to automatically delete cookies, when being closed. The deactivation of cookies can limit the functionality of this website.

The usage of cookies, which are necessary for digital communication processes and to provide you with wanted features (e.g. shopping cart), is in accordance to Article 6 (1) lit. f GDPR. As provider of this website, we do have a justified interest in using cookies to enable an error free and smooth experience of our website.

Data processing by plugins

YouTube

This website uses plugins from Youtube for the integration and display of video content. Provider of the video portal is YouTube, LLC, 901 Cherry Ave., San Bruno, CA 94066, USA.

We use the advanced data protection mode, which, according to information from the provider, saves user data only after playing the video. In the moment, when the embedded video is being played back, Youtube uses cookies to collect information about user behaviour.

When a page with an embedded Youtube plugin is being visited, a connection to the servers of YouTube will be established. Through this connection, YouTube learns, which pages you visited.

YouTube can match your online behaviour to your personal profile, if you are logged into your YouTube account. You can prevent this by logging out of your account.

The usage of YouTube is in the interest of an appealing appearance of our website. This is a justified interest in accordance to Article 6 (1) lit. f GDPR.

You can find details on the handling of user data in the privacy policy of Youtube: https://www.google.com/intl/en/policies/privacy.

Vimeo

This website uses plugins from Vimeo for the integration and display of video content. Provider of the video portal is Vimeo Inc., 555 West 18th Street, New York, New York 10011, USA.

When a page with an embedded Vimeo plugin is being visited, a connection to the servers of Vimeo will be established. Through this connection, Vimeo learns, which pages you visited. Vimeo learns your IP address, even if you are not logged into the video portal or do not have an account. The gathered information by Vimeo is being tansferred to the servers of the video portal in the USA.

Vimeo can match your online behaviour to your personal profile, if you are logged into your Vimeo account. You can prevent this by logging out of your account.

You can find details on the handling of user data in the privacy policy of Vimeo: https://vimeo.com/privacy.
